Overly restrictive inclusion criteria

Sponsors write criteria that eliminate 90% of eligible patients without knowing it — until site activation, when it's too late and too expensive to change.

Protocol amendments mid-trial

Criteria changes after launch trigger 6–12 month delays, IRB resubmissions, and budget overruns. Three DMD confirmatory trials failed in 2024–2025 alone.

No simulation before commitment

Biotechs commit to sites and $50M budgets without knowing how many qualifying patients actually exist in the real patient population.

Placebo arm ethics in pediatric rare disease

Randomizing children with DMD to placebo while they rapidly deteriorate is ethically untenable — but required by standard trial design.
